Our Mission

Transforming wound care documentation to give providers more time with patients and less time with paperwork

The Problem We Lived

OmniWound EMR was born from real frustration experienced by our founder, Dr. Diane Lusas, during her time as Medical Director at Integrated Wound Care (IWC). While traveling between facilities in Connecticut, she encountered the same documentation challenges that plague wound care specialists nationwide.

Traditional EMR systems forced providers through endless clicks, unintuitive screen flows, and required bouncing between different software sections just to complete basic encounter documentation. Even worse, these systems weren't designed for traveling providers, making facility-level scheduling nearly impossible.

Dr. Lusas knew there had to be a better way. In 2019, she partnered with experienced software engineers to create the EMR she wished she had – one built specifically for the unique workflows of traveling wound care specialists.

Our Journey

From identifying the problem to creating the solution that's transforming wound care documentation

2016-2018

The Frustration Builds

Dr. Diane Lusas, appointed Medical Director at Integrated Wound Care in 2017, travels between multiple facilities in Connecticut providing wound care. She experiences firsthand the inefficiencies of traditional EMR systems – too many clicks, unintuitive workflows, and poor support for mobile providers.

2019

The Solution Begins

Dr. Lusas partners with experienced software engineers to create the EMR she wished she had. Development begins on a platform designed specifically for traveling wound care specialists, informed by her real-world experience with EMR pain points.

2020

Platform Launch

OmniWound EMR officially launches with features designed around the actual workflows of traveling wound care providers. Early adopters immediately report significant improvements in documentation efficiency.

2021-2023

Proven Results

Word spreads through the wound care community as providers experience up to 50% efficiency improvements. The platform scales to serve dozens of practices while maintaining the streamlined workflow that made it successful.

2024-2025

Industry Leadership

Today, OmniWound EMR serves 100+ providers processing over 4,000 patient encounters weekly. Dr. Lusas continues practicing wound care, ensuring our platform evolves with real-world provider needs.

Meet Our Founder

Diane Lusas, MD, AAPWCA

Founder & Medical Director

Dr. Lusas completed her general surgery residency at Yale New Haven Hospital and earned her medical degree from New York Medical College. She graduated Summa Cum Laude from Northeastern University with a B.S. in Biochemistry.
