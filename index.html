Why Choose OmniWound EMR?

🚗

For Traveling Providers

Purpose-built for mobile wound care specialists. Smart scheduling maximizes patient visits per facility while optimizing travel routes. Access patient records and complete documentation seamlessly from any location.

⚡

Fast Documentation

Eliminate excessive clicking with our streamlined interface. Automated, detailed notes are instantly generated for provider review, editing, and approval.

🤖

AI-Powered Coding & Billing

Automated ICD-10 coding reduces errors and ensures accurate billing every time. Comprehensive billing services available as an add-on feature.

🛡️

Secure Cloud Platform

HIPAA-compliant cloud infrastructure with native mobile app. Enterprise-grade security ensures your data is protected and accessible from anywhere.

📊

Real-Time Analytics

Track patient outcomes, monitor healing progress, and generate reports that help improve care quality and operational efficiency.

🔗

Full Interoperability

Connect seamlessly with existing EHRs, practice management systems, labs, billing platforms, and FHIR-compliant systems. Import/export patient data without disruption to your current workflow.

MIPS Ready

Built-in MIPS reporting and quality measures tracking. Automatically capture and submit required data for maximum reimbursement incentives.
