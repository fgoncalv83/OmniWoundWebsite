📰 Industry News Roundup

Medicare Proposes Major Cost Controls on Wound Care Products
CMS is proposing to reclassify skin substitutes (used in wound care) to be paid as “incident-to” supplies, which could cut Medicare spending by ~90% starting in 2026. Providers may face new documentation and reimbursement challenges.

FY 2026 SNF Medicare Rate Increase: 3.2% Bump
CMS finalized a 3.2% increase in Skilled Nursing Facility PPS rates for FY 2026—adding $1.16 billion compared to FY 2025. Updates also include ICD-10 mapping changes and new quality reporting requirements.
